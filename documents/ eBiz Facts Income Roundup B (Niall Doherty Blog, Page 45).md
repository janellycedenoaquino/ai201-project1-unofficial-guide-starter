#### This Local Newsletter Earned $52K Last Month (85% Profit)

![Michael Kauffman](https://i.gr-assets.com/images/S/compressed.photo.goodreads.com/hostedimages/1739300410i/36557288.jpg) Michael KauffmanFounder of Catskill Crew $52,000 revenue in 1 month

[Catskill Crew](https://catskillcrew.com/) is a newsletter for The Catskills, a mountainous region in the eastern USA ![🇺🇸](https://i.gr-assets.com/images/S/compressed.photo.goodreads.com/hostedimages/1712642428i/35396452.png) popular for hiking and such.

Michael Kauffman started the newsletter around [the end of 2023](https://x.com/MikeyPesto/status/1886888638828454079) and recently [tweeted](https://x.com/MikeyPesto/status/1885397629875441742) that it earned $52K in January (85% profit).

[A recent edition of the newsletter](https://catskillcrew.com/p/catskill-crew-068) ![👀](https://i.gr-assets.com/images/S/compressed.photo.goodreads.com/hostedimages/1713001341i/35410001.png)

Michael also [tweeted](https://x.com/MikeyPesto/status/1884586393508139502) recently…

> You can monetize ANY newsletter without ads. Yesterday I did $3,900 in revenue. $1,600 in D2C and $2,300 in B2B.
> 
> No ads. No cold outreach. No media kits. No CPMs. No CPCs. Just product. Build an audience and validate ideas.

Also says in the replies there that they have 17.5K subscribers now and sell “board games and discount cards.”

Products in [their store](https://catskillcrew.myshopify.com/collections/all) include the [Catskill-opoly board game](https://catskillcrew.myshopify.com/products/catskillopoly-board-game) ($60 a pop) ![😎](https://i.gr-assets.com/images/S/compressed.photo.goodreads.com/hostedimages/1712917406i/35406998.png)

Love seeing a non-city local newsletter doing so much revenue.

Could you do something similar for another region? ![🤔](https://i.gr-assets.com/images/S/compressed.photo.goodreads.com/hostedimages/1712432897i/35388361.png)

#### He’s Launched 20 Startups With This Framework

John Rush [shares](https://x.com/johnrushx/status/1882277087224185312) the ideation framework he’s used to launch over 20 startups…

![](https://i.gr-assets.com/images/S/compressed.photo.goodreads.com/hostedimages/1738840943i/36530576._SX540_.png)

Jamji Jamaramji breaks it down like this ![👇](https://i.gr-assets.com/images/S/compressed.photo.goodreads.com/hostedimages/1712545869i/35393091.png)

> start working on a work project until you run into a big pain point that slows you down. Solve that. Turn that solution into a business.
> 
> Examples:  
> Struggle @ website -> Website solution  
> Annoyed @ Analytics -> Analytics solution  
> Struggle @ SEO -> SEO solution

So any time you have a problem and struggle to find a solution = business opportunity ![💡](https://i.gr-assets.com/images/S/compressed.photo.goodreads.com/hostedimages/1712568944i/35393901.png)

Especially if the problem is work/business related.

#### Bought The Site 3 Months Ago, Just Sold For $100K+

![Tim Bennetto](https://i.gr-assets.com/images/S/compressed.photo.goodreads.com/hostedimages/1696761998i/34800979.png) Tim BennettoSerial Entrepreneur $100,000 revenue from website flip

Tim Bennetto [tweeted](https://x.com/Timb03/status/1846103852590027200) back in October…

> Just bought this app: [colormagic.app](https://colormagic.app/)
> 
> Gets 500K pageviews/mo ![🤯](https://i.gr-assets.com/images/S/compressed.photo.goodreads.com/hostedimages/1712254769i/35381756.png)
> 
> Also ranks for some really high difficulty KW’s.

Tim didn’t disclose the purchase price but said it “was pretty cheap.”

Then at the end of January he [tweeted](https://x.com/Timb03/status/1884415769058107838) …

> Just sold Color Magic for six figures! ![🤯](https://i.gr-assets.com/images/S/compressed.photo.goodreads.com/hostedimages/1712254769i/35381756.png)
> 
> – acquired 3 mo ago…  
> – rebuilt, optimized seo…  
> – added pages, features  
> – 2M palettes generated with ai  
> – put ads to generate revenue  
> – 5x traffic to 1.6M views/mo

I’d guess he bought it for $30K, but even at $50K he would have doubled his money in a little over 3 months ![💰](https://i.gr-assets.com/images/S/compressed.photo.goodreads.com/hostedimages/1713058225i/35412212.png)

Tim found the site by searching for keywords on Ahrefs, then [reaching out to the owner](https://x.com/Timb03/status/1846121559322132768) …

> Contacted plenty, only small % respond. Best way to get a good deal though.

You could try this for a niche you’re interested in.

![🧐](https://i.gr-assets.com/images/S/compressed.photo.goodreads.com/hostedimages/1713526610i/35430324.png) Find an undervalued / under monetized site via keyword research, see if you can buy, improve, and flip within a few months.

#### His Humble Agency Earned $132K Last Year From Lithuania
