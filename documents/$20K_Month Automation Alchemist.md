#### $20K/Month Automation Alchemist

\[image error\]Mike CardonaAutomation Expert $20,000 per month  
Published December 5, 2022 by [Niall Doherty](https://ebizfacts.com/about/niall/)  

Mike Cardona calls himself an Automation Alchemist, and [shares](https://twitter.com/CSMikeCardona/status/1593521895994929153) how he makes $20,000 per month.

First he built his automation and marketing skills by growing a newsletter for a year. He then got busy helping people for free and collecting social proof.

Third:

> Posted free automation tutorials for six months & positioned myself as an “SME” on LinkedIn (70% revenue/clients)

Mike apparently charges a minimum of $3000 per client, and an average of $8000.

> If they didn’t recoup their investment, I’d give them their $ back… I haven’t had to return any money to date. (Thankfully).

Check out [Mike’s LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/mikeacardona/recent-activity/shares/) to see the kind of content he posts that leads to client work. And as he notes [here](https://twitter.com/CSMikeCardona/status/1593522812093751296), the engagement on those posts can be misleading. Some of the posts that have generated lots of revenue for him got very little engagement.
